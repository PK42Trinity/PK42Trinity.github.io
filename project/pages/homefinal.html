90's Video Game Innovations

The What

What this site is, is a brief look into what some of the significant innovations that can be seen in video games made in the 90's and how they were innovative by giving examples through the video games themselves of how they protrayed these innovations.

The Why

I do this so that anyone interested be they a gamer or not can take the chance to see some of the innovations that video game developers have come up with that make their games better and that lay the groundwork for making even better games in the future as well.
