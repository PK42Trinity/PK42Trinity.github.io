Wolfenstein Texture Mapping

Wolfenstein 3D

Why it's Innovative

First person shooters were constantly redeveloping the possibilities of 3D technology. "Wolfenstein 3D" was one of the first video games to feature texture mapping where graphical textures are wrapped around 3D objects.
